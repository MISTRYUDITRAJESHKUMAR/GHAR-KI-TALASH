Terms & Conditions

GharDhundho — Zero Brokerage Real Estate Platform

1. Free Service: GharDhundho ek free platform hai. Koi brokerage ya commission nahi liya jaata.

2. Real Listings Only: User sirf apni khud ki property list kar sakta hai. Broker ya dalal ki taraf se multiple listings allowed nahi hain. Ek user sirf ek active ad rakh sakta hai.

3. Direct Contact: Buyers/renters directly WhatsApp pe contact karte hain. GharDhundho koi responsibility nahi leta transactions ke liye.

4. Accurate Information: Users ko sahi location, price aur contact details deni hogi. Galat information pe account suspend ho sakta hai.

5. Data Privacy: Aapka data safely Firebase mein store hota hai. Aapka WhatsApp number sirf logged-in users dekh sakte hain.

6. India Only: Yeh platform sirf India ke liye hai. Sirf Indian pincodes allowed hain.

7. Content Moderation: Koi bhi inappropriate content allowed nahi. GharDhundho kisi bhi ad ko bina notice ke remove kar sakta hai.

8. Governing Law: Yeh terms Indian law ke under governed hain.

Privacy Policy

Data Collection: Hum Google login se aapka naam, email aur photo collect karte hain.

WhatsApp Number: Aapka WhatsApp number property ad ke saath store hota hai aur sirf logged-in users dekh sakte hain.

Location Data: Nearby properties dikhane ke liye aapki location use hoti hai — yeh locally processed hoti hai.

Firebase: Sab data Google Firebase mein securely store hota hai.

No Selling: Aapka data kabhi bhi third parties ko nahi becha jaata.

Cookies: Hum sirf login session ke liye browser storage use karte hain.
